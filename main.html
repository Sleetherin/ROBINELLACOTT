Robin Venetia Ellacott was born on October 9, 1984 in Masham, North Yorkshire, UK. She has three brothers: Stephen, Martin, Jonathan and no sisters. Her parents are Linda and Michael Ellacott. She likes tea and wine and crips, so when you visit her do make sure you have three of these with you. Robin always wanted to be a detective.She started studying Psychology but dropped out of university.She started her detective career by working for Temporary Solutions at Strike's detective agency, and after displaying her talents Cormoran Strike decided to permanetely hire her as his secretary/assisant. Robin Ellacott then took some courses in surveillance and counter-surveillance. Now,she is a paid partner in the agency, which was renamed Strike&Ellacott Agency.
